budonotebook.click is now closed. budonotebook.click je zatvoren.

Why we're closing Zašto zatvaramo

For six months, budonotebook.click was online — a space built for aikido practitioners to track their training, prepare for exams, and connect over technique.

Two users registered. Neither logged in again.

The website was quiet. But the lesson was clear.

Aikido is wonderfully diverse. Each club, school, and lineage carries its own curriculum, its own language, its own way. Even within Aikikai, the exam programmes differ from dojo to dojo — from Hombu's standards to Iwama's weapons-integrated tradition. Building one platform that fits everyone turned out to be the wrong tool for the job.

Personal practice is personal. It deserves a personal space.

Šest mjeseci, budonotebook.click bio je dostupan — prostor izgrađen za aikido praktičare za praćenje treninga, pripremu ispita i dijeljenje tehnika.

Dvoje korisnika se registriralo. Nitko se nije vratio prijaviti.

Web stranica je bila tiha. Ali pouka je bila jasna.

Aikido je divno raznolik. Svaki klub, svaka škola i svaka linija nosi vlastiti nastavni plan, vlastiti jezik i vlastiti put. Čak i unutar Aikikai-ja, ispitni programi razlikuju se od dojoa do dojoa — od standarda Hombu-a do tradicije Iwamiške škole s integracijom oružja. Izgradnja jedne platforme koja odgovara svima pokazala se pogrešnim pristupom.

Osobna praksa je osobna. Zaslužuje osobni prostor.

A better path forward Bolji put naprijed

We've created Budo Notebook — a free, open-source template vault for Obsidian, a free offline note-taking app that works with plain files.

Download it, open it, and make it yours. Adjust the exam programme to match your dojo. Add your language. Log your training. Record your sensei's corrections. Build your own technique library with video links.

No account. No server. No one-size-fits-all. Just your practice, your way.

Stvorili smo Budo Notebook — besplatni, open-source predložak vaulta za Obsidian, besplatnu offline aplikaciju za bilješke koja radi s običnim datotekama.

Preuzmi ga, otvori i prilagodi sebi. Izmijeni ispitni program prema svom dojou. Dodaj vlastiti jezik. Bilježi treninge. Zapisuj ispravke svog senseija. Gradi vlastitu knjižnicu tehnika s video linkovima.

Bez računa. Bez servera. Bez kompromisa. Samo tvoja praksa, na tvoj način.
